Bolotnaya Online is a set of public pages with live cameras and an archive of frames. There are no contact forms, user accounts, dashboards, or comments.

## Personal data

The project does not collect or process personal data of visitors.

## Cookies and analytics

The site uses Yandex.Metrica for visit statistics. The counter may use cookies and collect anonymized technical parameters such as device type, screen resolution, browser settings, and referrer.

Yandex describes its approach to sensitive data here: [Yandex.Metrica and confidential data](https://yandex.ru/support/metrica/ru/general/confidential-data).

If you do not want cookies, you can disable them in your browser settings. In that case, some analytics features may not work properly.

## Hosting
