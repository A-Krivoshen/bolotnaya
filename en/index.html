FAQ

What is Bolotnaya Online?

An independent open-source project by a Yakimanka resident: cameras, history and photos of Bolotnaya Square. Not mass media or an official city site.

Why are cameras offline?

Facade maintenance is underway. Status updates are published in the blog.
